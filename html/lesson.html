Lessons Learnt

We learned a lot, some of which is hard to put into words. Here's what we can say:

  • Don't focus on "how" at the beginning - this will just lead to picking favorites later in concept generation
  • Many aspects of systems engineering, such as the House of Quality or the Pugh Matrix, seem daunting at first but are very straightforward once you just sit down and start.
  • Don't select a final concept before you have reviewed your needs analysis
  • Before anything else, make sure everyone is in agreement on your mission
