Next Steps

Project Spillcry is far from over. What exists now is a high-level concept, but there are many details to be further developed and problems that we most likely have not even thought of yet. To list some unanswered questions:

  • What will the drone look like, specifically?
  • Will drones be bought from an existing vendor or will a brand new design be used?
  • What sort of cameras will be used?
  • What sort of software will be used for computer vision and linear Gaussian approximation? Will any existing code be utilized?
  • How will the different components of the system (UAVs, computer vision software, linear Gaussian approximation software, communication software) all be linked together into one cohesive system?
  • What will be the final "sticker price" of the entire system?
